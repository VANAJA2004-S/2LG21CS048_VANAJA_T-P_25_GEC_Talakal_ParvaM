A farmer preparing the soil is a crucial step in ensuring a healthy and productive crop. The process begins with clearing the land by removing weeds, rocks, and any leftover crop residues.

Sowing is the process of planting seeds in prepared soil to grow crops. After the soil is properly prepared, the farmer selects high-quality seeds suited for the season and soil type. The seeds can be sown manually by hand, using a traditional tool, or with modern seed drills for even distribution.

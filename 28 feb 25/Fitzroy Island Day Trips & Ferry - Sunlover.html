Best of Island Package

Adult $175  |  Child $117  |  Family $467

Fitzroy Island, nestled in the Great Barrier Reef near Cairns, epitomises tropical paradise with its pristine beaches and vibrant marine life. Sunlover’s “Best of Island” package offers a comprehensive experience showcasing the island’s natural beauty and recreational opportunities.

Guests can start their journey with convenient return boat transfers, ensuring a hassle-free arrival and departure. The package includes a sumptuous premium buffet lunch at Zephyrs, the renowned restaurant at Fitzroy Island Resort, where diners can savour delicious meals amidst stunning ocean views.

For those eager to explore underwater wonders, the package includes a Glass Bottom Boat tour, providing a mesmerizing glimpse into the colourful marine ecosystem without getting wet. Adventurers can further immerse themselves in the reef’s splendour with complimentary snorkelling equipment, including masks, fins, snorkels, buoyancy vests, and lycra suits.

Whether relaxing on the island’s sandy shores or embarking on aquatic adventures, Sunlover’s package ensures that every guest experiences the best of Fitzroy Island’s natural treasures and hospitality, making it an unforgettable tropical getaway.

Do’s and Don’ts on the island

  • Resort and resort pool is off limits unless you are a guest of the resort.
  • Do not touch any wildlife, on the island or in the water.
  • No standing on live coral.
  • No swimming/ kayaking/ paddle boarding past the boat channel markers.
  • Pick up your litter.
  • Bring plenty of water for the day, or purchase at Foxy’s.
  • Reef shoes are suggested (and available on board).

How to secure your belongings

  • Bring a waterproof bag.
  • Take waterproof bag with essentials on the kayak or paddleboard.
  • Cover your items on the beach with a towel to protect from sun.
  • Only bring what you need, leave jewellery and expensive items at home/hotel.

Safety issues

  • Every ocean has currents, if you are not a strong swimmer, stay near the beach in shallow water, request a buoyancy vest, let our staff know.
  • Stingers can be around all year, please wear a stinger suit, available on board for hire.
  • The island has high tide and low tide, please confirm best snorkelling times with crew before departing the vessel.
